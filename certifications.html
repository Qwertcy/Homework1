White-hat Hacking

Entry-Level

CompTIA Security+ – Covers basic security concepts, risk management, and network security; a common starting point for cybersecurity careers.
CompTIA CySA+ (Cybersecurity Analyst) – Focuses on behavioral analytics, threat detection, and incident response.


Intermediate-Level

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) – Covers penetration testing techniques, ethical hacking concepts, and cybersecurity tools.
EC-Council Certified Security Analyst (ECSA) – Builds upon CEH with hands-on penetration testing skills.


Advanced-Level

Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) – One of the most recognized certifications covering security policies, risk management, and architecture.
Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) – A hands-on penetration testing certification requiring deep exploit development skills.


Specialized

GIAC Penetration Tester (GPEN) – Concentrates on advanced penetration testing skills.
GIAC Reverse Engineering Malware (GREM) – Specializes in malware analysis and forensic investigations.
